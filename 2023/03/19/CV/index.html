Research skills

Technical

During my master, I have built up a wealth of techniques including:

  • Molecular/Cell Biology: Highly experiments literate able to RNA and DNA extraction, PCR, CRISPR/Cas9, western blotting, Arabidopsis transformation, confocal microscopy, disease resistance assay.
  • Bioinformatics techniques: Familiar with R and python, able to RNA-seq data analysis, untargeted metabolism data analysis, genome-wide association analysis(GWAS), and genome population analysis.
  • Analytical techniques: Quantify the endogenous level of SA by LC-MS.
